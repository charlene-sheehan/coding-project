A little about me.

My life is spent between my life-long home in Southern California and newfound home in Madrid, Spain. My journey into tech started with a young-life crisis after my first year in Spain. I loved teaching in Spain, but for many reasons, found that it wasn't a feasible option for me in the near future. While musing about starting a blog, I stumbled into all the opportunities that there are in tech.

With nothing to lose and only coding skills to gain, I dove in headfirst with the Skillcrush online coding school. The founders of the company inspire me to build my future div by div while I'm still teaching kiddos English in Spain. While design is cool, I think my passion lies somewhere in front-end or back-end development, or maybe both! This website serves as my portfolio while I, as millenials do, take time to figure myself out.
